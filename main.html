Summary

The Shawshank Redemption follows Andy Dufresne, a banker sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for a crime he insists he didn't commit. Over decades he forms a powerful friendship with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding, quietly planning a life of hope, redemption, and — ultimately — escape.

Actors

Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne — a calm, resourceful banker whose inner resolve and intelligence help him survive and change the lives around him.

Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding — the film's narrator and Andy's trusted friend, wise and grounded, who learns to hope again.

Favorite Quote

"Get busy living, or get busy dying." — Andy Dufresne. This line captures the movie’s central theme of choosing hope and action even in bleak times.
