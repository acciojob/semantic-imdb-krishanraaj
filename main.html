Movie Summary

The Shawshank Redemption follows Andy Dufresne, a banker sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, a crime he insists he didn't commit. Over the years, he befriends Ellis "Red" Redding, and together they experience the harsh realities of prison life. Andy uses his intelligence and hope to find freedom in an unexpected way.
